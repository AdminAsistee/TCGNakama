About TCGNakama

Japan's marketplace for authentic Pokémon, One Piece, and Magic: The Gathering single cards — priced at market value, shipped next day.

🃏 Who We Are

TCGNakama is a Japan-based trading card game marketplace specialising in Pokémon TCG, One Piece TCG, and Magic: The Gathering singles. Every card in our inventory is sourced, inspected, and authenticated before being listed for sale.

We operate out of Japan, giving collectors and investors worldwide direct access to Japanese market cards at real-time JPY prices — no middlemen, no markups.

🔍 Card Conditions

Every card is graded by our team before listing. Here's what each grade means:

NM

Near Mint

Essentially perfect. No visible wear, scratches, or whitening on edges. Straight from pack or sleeved immediately.

LP

Lightly Played

Minor edge wear or very light surface scratching. Still tournament-legal and sleeve-playable.

MP

Moderately Played

Noticeable wear, some edge whitening. Functional but shows signs of play.

Raw

Raw / Ungraded

Condition not individually assessed. Ideal for bulk buyers or those interested in PSA/BGS grading submission.
