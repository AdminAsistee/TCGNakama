grading TCG Condition Guide

📦 Package Type

inventory_2
Booster Pack

Factory-sealed booster pack. Card is inside the original packaging and has never been opened.

credit_card
Raw

Single card without protective casing. Stored in a penny sleeve or bare. Not professionally graded.

shield
Toploader

Card protected in a rigid plastic toploader case. Good for shipping and storage. Not professionally graded.

verified
Slab

Professionally graded and encapsulated in a tamper-proof case by PSA, BGS, CGC, or similar authority.

🃏 Card Condition

NM
Near Mint

Card is in excellent condition with minimal to no visible wear. Corners are sharp and surfaces are clean.

LP
Lightly Played

Minor edge wear or light scratching visible on close inspection. Still great for collecting.

MP
Moderately Played

Noticeable wear including scuffing, edge whitening, or light creasing. Good for casual play.

HP
Heavily Played

Significant wear including creases, heavy whitening, or surface damage. Suitable for play decks only.

DMG
Damaged

Major damage including tears, water damage, or missing pieces. Card identity still recognizable.

Raw
Raw / Ungraded

Card has not been professionally graded. Condition assessed by the seller.
